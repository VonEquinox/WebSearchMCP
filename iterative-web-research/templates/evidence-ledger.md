## Notes

- Use `Bucket` to group sources into direct evidence, mechanistic evidence, mitigation evidence, counterevidence / caveats, or surveys for orientation.
- Use `Evidence type` to distinguish **direct**, **adjacent**, or **framing** evidence.
- Use `Strength` to record **strong**, **moderate**, or **lower weight** evidence.
- Mark `Fetched` and `Verified` separately. A source can be fetched but not yet verified.
